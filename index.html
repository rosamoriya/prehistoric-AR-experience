T-Rex

One of the largest meat-eating dinosaurs, T. rex was the top predator of its time. It had a super strong bite and sharp teeth to hunt other dinosaurs, possibly including Triceratops. It lived in North America and Asia during the Late Cretaceous period.

Triceratops

This was a huge, plant-eating dinosaur with three horns on its face and a bony frill around its neck. It lived in North America during the Late Cretaceous period. Triceratops probably used its horns and frill to show off or protect itself.

Mammoth

A giant, plant-eating mammal that lived in cold climates. It had long, curved tusks, thick fur, and a layer of fat to keep warm. Woolly mammoths lived during the Ice Age and went extinct about 10,000 years ago.
